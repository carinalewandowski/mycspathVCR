About MyCSPath


The goal of MyCSPath is to eliminate the disparity between those who are interested in computer science and those who are actually compelled to enroll in COS or computer science-related courses by providing a platform for creating a multi-semester course path through computer science education. Furthermore, MyCSPath will enable Princeton students of diverse concentrations to make computer science a part of their academic experience in a way that best fulfills their individual needs and interests in an effort to make the broader discipline of computer science accessible to a wider audience of students.
