About MyCSPath


The goal of MyCSPath is to eliminate the disparity between those who are interested in computer science and those who are actually compelled to enroll in COS or computer science-related courses by aiding interdisciplinary course discovery. MyCSPath makes it easy to create multi-semester course paths through computer science education that best fulfill students' individual needs and interests, thus enabling Princeton students of diverse concentrations to make computer science a part of their academic experience. Furthermore, MyCSPath will hopefully contribute to making the broader discipline of computer science more accessible.
