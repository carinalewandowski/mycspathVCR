About MyCSPath

The goal of my project is to eliminate the disparity between those who are interested in computer science and those who are actually compelled to enroll in COS or computer science-related courses by providing an interest-based, goal-oriented platform for creating a multi-semester course path in computer science education. Furthermore, MyCOSPath will enable Princeton students of diverse concentrations to make computer science a part of their academic experience in a way that best fulfills their individual needs and interests. This objective is important as it aims to make the broader discipline of computer science accessible to a wider audience of students, as well as provide these students with an opportunity to learn computer science skills through a more manageable approach that works with their current skill sets and ambitions.
